# Microwave Door Switch Problem

## Overview
Door safety switch malfunctions. Prevents microwave from operating (stuck open) or keeps running with door open (stuck closed). Safety hazard if stuck closed. Caused by mechanical switch wear, electrical contact failure, or alignment issue.

## Symptoms
- Microwave won't start even with door closed
- Microwave continues running when door opened
- Door seems stuck or very hard to close
- Light stays on even with door closed
- Error code displayed
- Humming sound but no cooking
- Door latch feels weak

## Possible Causes
**Worn Switch Contacts**: Metal contacts corroded/worn. Cannot make proper electrical connection.

**Misaligned Door": Door no longer presses switch button correctly. Weak or no contact.

**Failed Switch Mechanism": Switch solenoid or mechanical latch broken.

**Stuck Switch Button": Button jammed by debris or mechanical wear. Cannot press or release.

**Burnt Contacts": Electrical arc burned switch contacts. Cannot conduct electricity properly.

**Loose Switch": Switch housing loose. Button doesn't engage properly.

## DIY Troubleshooting Steps

1. **Inspect door alignment": Close door fully. Look at gaps around frame. Even gaps all sides? Uneven gap = misalignment.

2. **Check door latch": Open door. Look at latch mechanism (small plastic piece that goes into notch). Does it appear bent or damaged?

3. **Feel for switch button": At door frame, find small protruding button (where door pushes to close). Press with finger. Should click. Does it click or feel stuck?

4. **Clean switch area": Debris around switch prevents proper contact. Wipe switch area gently with dry cloth.

5. **Check switch contacts": If accessible (requires opening microwave), look at switch contacts. Are they corroded (green/white oxidation) or blackened (burnt)?

6. **Try different door positions": Open door, close door several times. Press and release carefully. Does switch respond? Inconsistent response = wear.

## Safety Warnings
**Radiation Hazard": If switch stuck in "closed" position, microwave will run with door open. Microwave radiation hazard. Never operate with door open.

**Electrical": Switch carries full electrical load. Do not touch switch contacts directly.

## When To Call A Technician
- Switch not responding at all
- Contacts visibly burnt or corroded
- Door alignment severely off
- Switch mechanism broken

## Estimated Repair Cost (India)
- Door switch replacement: ₹1200–₹2500

## Preventive Maintenance
- Close door gently (don't slam)
- Clean door frame area monthly
- Keep switch button area clean and debris-free
- Don't force door if resistance felt
- Test switch monthly (open/close door, listen for click)
