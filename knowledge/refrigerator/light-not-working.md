# Refrigerator Light Not Working

## Overview
Interior light fails to turn on when door opens or remains always off. Light bulb dead, socket defective, or switch mechanism malfunctioning. Does not affect cooling but reduces convenience and suggests possible electrical fault in door system.

## Symptoms
- Light doesn't turn on when door opens
- Light always on (door switch not working)
- Light flickers intermittently
- Light very dim
- Bulb hot or burnt smell near socket

## Possible Causes
**Dead Bulb**: Most common cause. Filament burnt out after normal lifespan (1000-2000 hours).

**Wrong Bulb Type**: Incorrect wattage or type installed. Some bulbs not compatible with fridge socket.

**Faulty Door Switch**: Mechanical switch that triggers light doesn't engage when door opens.

**Defective Socket**: Socket terminals corroded or broken. Cannot make electrical contact with bulb.

**Electrical Short**: Internal wiring fault prevents light circuit from functioning.

**Thermal Overload**: Light circuit breaker tripped (rare).

## DIY Troubleshooting Steps

1. **Safety first**: Unplug fridge before replacing bulb or accessing socket.

2. **Remove dead bulb**: Open fridge. Locate light bulb (usually top center or upper corner). Turn counterclockwise to remove bulb.

3. **Inspect bulb**: Check if filament is broken (visible grey powder inside = burnt). If clearly burnt, bulb is dead.

4. **Check bulb type**: Note wattage and type (e.g., 40W, E14 socket, incandescent). Verify it matches fridge manual specification.

5. **Install new bulb**: Use correct bulb type and wattage. Screw in gently until snug. Do not overtighten.

6. **Replug fridge**: Plug back in. Open door slowly. Does light turn on?

7. **Test door switch**: Open door fully. Look for small protruding button on door frame (not door itself). Press it gently with finger. Light should turn off. Release—light should turn back on. If no response, switch is defective.

8. **Check socket**: If bulb is new and correct type but light doesn't work, look inside socket. Any corrosion or debris? Very carefully clean socket terminals with dry cloth (unplugged).

## Safety Warnings
**Electrical**: Fridge light circuit operates at lower voltage (usually 120V or less) but always verify unplugged before working.

**Bulb Heat**: Bulb gets hot during operation. Allow 5 minutes cooling before touching new bulb after operation.

## When To Call A Technician
- Door switch not responding (requires replacement)
- Socket corroded or damaged (requires replacement)
- Light circuit breaker tripped (electrical fault)
- Wiring inside door panel damaged (rare, structural issue)

## Estimated Repair Cost (India)
- Light bulb replacement: ₹30–₹100
- Door switch replacement: ₹600–₹1200
- Socket replacement: ₹400–₹800
- Wiring repair: ₹1000–₹2000

## Preventive Maintenance
- Replace bulb when dimming observed (before complete failure)
- Ensure correct bulb type installed (verify manual)
- Keep door switch clean (no debris in mechanism)
- Don't force door open/closed (stresses switch)
